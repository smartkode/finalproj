Legal Investigations Virtual Environment

This system aims to replace the current process and manual system used by the JCF for logging reports, case investigations, records and offenses.

L.I.V.E.  is geared at providing support for and increasing the efficiency of law enforcement, legal and investigative operations and services

It also provides an analytic self search feature which identifies trends between data objects and reports them to the appropriate users.
